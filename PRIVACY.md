# AutoSnooze Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 17, 2026_

AutoSnooze is a bedside clock app. It does not collect, store, or transmit
any personal data.

- **No data collection.** The app has no analytics, no advertising, no
  tracking, and no third-party SDKs.
- **No network access.** The app never connects to the internet. Everything
  it does happens entirely on your device.
- **Local settings only.** Your alarm times, chosen sounds, and display
  brightness are saved only on your device, in the app's private storage,
  and are deleted when you delete the app.

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue at
<https://github.com/wcharliebrown/AutoSnooze/issues>.
