**Description:**
AutoSnooze turns your iPhone into a classic bedside clock: big red
7-segment LED digits on a pure black background, drawn like a real LED
panel — faint ghost segments included.

Made for the nightstand:

- The screen stays on while the app is open — no sleep, no lock
- Tap the display for a brightness slider; dim it to a whisper for dark
  bedrooms (your level is remembered)
- Works in portrait and landscape
- Follows your 12/24-hour setting, with a small AM/PM indicator
- No ads, no tracking, no network access — nothing but a clock

Two gentle alarms:

- Each with its own time, on/off switch, and sound (Chime, Gong, Bell,
  or Beep)
- An alarm plays its sound once and stops. No snooze, no repeats, no
  "dismiss" dance at 7 AM — it respects that you heard it
- Alarms sound even if the silent switch is on

Note: AutoSnooze is a bedside clock, not a background alarm app. Alarms
fire while the app is open on your nightstand — leave it running on the
charger overnight, as intended.

**App Review Information → Notes** (paste this so the reviewer understands
the design):
AutoSnooze is a bedside night clock, meant to stay open on a nightstand
all night. By design, the screen never sleeps while the app is in the
foreground, and alarms fire only while the app is open. When an alarm
fires it plays its sound exactly once — no repeat and no snooze, so no
dismiss UI is needed. To test: tap the gear (bottom right), enable Alarm
1 a minute or two ahead, and keep the app in the foreground. Tap the
display to reveal the brightness slider.
